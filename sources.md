# Evidence-Based Citations and Sources

## Policy Research Sources
The "Author-Authored" policy pack for the Purple Merit project is synthesized from the following industry-leading e-commerce standards and legal frameworks:

1. **Amazon Help - Returns & Refunds**
   - URL: [https://www.amazon.com/gp/help/customer/display.html?nodeId=GKM69DUUYQW97798](https://www.amazon.com/gp/help/customer/display.html?nodeId=GKM69DUUYQW97798)
   - Topics: Standard 30-day window, Marketplace seller rules, high-value electronics.
   - Date Accessed: March 30, 2026

2. **European Commission - Consumer Guarantees, Misleading Advertising, and Returns**
   - URL: [https://europa.eu/youreurope/citizens/consumers/shopping/guarantees-returns/index_en.htm](https://europa.eu/youreurope/citizens/consumers/shopping/guarantees-returns/index_en.htm)
   - Topics: 14-day Right of Withdrawal, international cross-border consumer rights.
   - Date Accessed: March 30, 2026

3. **Walmart - Returns Policy**
   - URL: [https://www.walmart.com/help/article/returns-policy/c5952d7ceca04938a417641f3e790103](https://www.walmart.com/help/article/returns-policy/c5952d7ceca04938a417641f3e790103)
   - Topics: Hygiene item rules, perishable items, seasonal holiday windows.
   - Date Accessed: March 30, 2026

4. **FedEx Service Guide - Terms and Conditions**
   - URL: [https://www.fedex.com/content/dam/fedex/us-united-states/services/Service_Guide_2024.pdf](https://www.fedex.com/content/dam/fedex/us-united-states/services/Service_Guide_2024.pdf)
   - Topics: Lost package claim windows (14 days), hazardous materials (batteries), photo evidence requirements.
   - Date Accessed: March 30, 2026

5. **Apple - Device Returns and Trade-ins**
   - URL: [https://www.apple.com/shop/help/returns_refund](https://www.apple.com/shop/help/returns_refund)
   - Topics: Data privacy, account removal requirements (Find My activation lock).
   - Date Accessed: March 30, 2026

## Methodology
The internal policies were authored to consolidate these varying public standards into a single, cohesive "Ground Truth" for the Purple Merit AI Assistant. This ensures the RAG system handles multi-category complexity (perishables + electronics) correctly.
