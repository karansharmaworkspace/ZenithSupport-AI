# International Logistics and Returns

## 1. Global Shipping Network
Our international network covers over 150 countries.
- **Estimated Transit:** 10 to 21 business days.
- **Customs Holds:** We are not responsible for delays caused by local customs authorities.
- **Taxes and Duties:** Prices displayed on the shop are exclusive of local import taxes (except for EU shipments). Customers are responsible for paying these upon arrival.

## 2. EU Right of Withdrawal (Statutory)
Under EU Directive 2011/83/EU, consumers in the European Union have a "Right of Withdrawal" from most online purchases.
- **Period:** 14 days from the date of physical receipt.
- **Refund Requirements:** The merchant must refund all payments received from the consumer, including delivery costs (except for the supplementary costs resulting from your choice of a type of delivery other than the least expensive type of standard delivery offered by us).
- **Return Shipping:** The cost of returning the goods is borne by the consumer unless the merchant has agreed to bear them.

## 3. Rest of World Return Rules
For countries outside the US and EU, standard return rules apply with a fixed 45-day window to allow for longer transit times.
- **Carrier Choice:** We strongly recommend using a courier with international tracking (DHL, FedEx, UPS). We cannot process refunds for international returns lost in transit without a tracking number.
