# Holiday and Seasonal Extensions

## 1. Holiday Return Window
Items purchased between November 1st and December 31st are eligible for return until January 31st of the following year.

## 2. Seasonal Items
Temporary outdoor items (pools, inflatable decorations, holiday lights) are subject to a 25% restocking fee if returned within the standard 30-day window but after the holiday/season has passed.

## 3. Price Matching
We do not offer price matching for "Black Friday" or "Cyber Monday" deals after an order has been placed.
