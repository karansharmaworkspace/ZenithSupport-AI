# Subscription Box Policy

## 1. Monthly Boxes
Subscription boxes are "Final Sale" and cannot be returned individually if you do not like the contents.
- **Requirement:** Only full, unopened boxes can be returned within 7 days of receipt.

## 2. Damage in Transit
If an item inside your subscription box arrives broken, we will ship a replacement for that specific item or offer a partial credit.

## 3. Skip/Cancel Rules
Subscription skips must be requested 48 hours before the billing date (the 1st of every month).
