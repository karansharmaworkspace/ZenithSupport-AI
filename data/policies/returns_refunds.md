# Returns and Refunds Policy (Detailed Version)

## 1. Standard Returns
Customers may return most new, unopened items sold and fulfilled by Purple Merit within **30 days** of delivery for a full refund. 
- **Timeframe:** The 30-day window starts from the date the tracking status shows "Delivered".
- **Condition:** Items must be in their original packaging, including all tags, manuals, and accessories.
- **Restocking Fee:** A 10% restocking fee may apply to high-value items (over $1,000) that are returned in opened-box condition.

## 2. Category-Specific Return Rules
### 2.1 Apparel and Footwear
- **Try-on Policy:** Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have all original tags attached.
- **Footwear:** Must be returned in the original branded shoe box, which must be placed inside a shipping box to avoid damage to the brand packaging.
- **Exceptions:** Custom-tailored or altered garments are non-returnable.

### 2.2 Health and Beauty
- **Condition:** Must be in original, unopened, and sealed packaging.
- **Sanitary Items:** Hairbrushes, makeup sponges, and opened perfumes are strictly non-returnable for health reasons.
- **Allergic Reactions:** If a customer experiences an allergic reaction, they must provide a photo of the reaction and the product batch number for a specialized health-safety refund.

### 2.3 Home and Kitchen
- **Large Appliances:** Must be inspected upon delivery. Once signed for, returns are only accepted for internal mechanical defects.
- **Small Appliances:** Can be returned within 30 days if unused. If used (e.g., coffee grounds found in machine), a 20% cleaning fee will be deducted.

### 2.4 Automotive Parts
- **Installation:** Parts that have been installed or show signs of installation (grease marks, bolt indentations) are non-returnable.
- **Tires:** Must be unmounted and unused.

## 3. Professional/Bulk Orders
Orders containing more than 20 units of a single SKU are considered "Bulk Orders".
- **Cancellation:** Bulk orders cannot be cancelled once they have entered the "Processing" stage.
- **Returns:** Bulk returns are subject to a 15% handling fee unless the items are defective.

## 4. Non-Returnable Items (Exceptions)
The following items cannot be returned:
- **Hygiene Items:** Undergarments, swimwear (if hygiene seal is broken), and personal care products.
- **Perishables:** Food, flowers, and live plants (unless arrived damaged).
- **Digital Content:** Downloadable software, gift cards, and prepaid game cards.
- **Customized Items:** Products with personalized engravings or custom-built configurations.

## 5. Refund Methods
- **Original Payment:** Refunds are normally processed back to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.
- **Store Credit:** Customers may opt for immediate Store Credit, which is added to the account balance instantly upon return verification.

## 6. Detailed Internal Adjudication Procedures
Agents must follow these steps for every return request:
1. Verify the order date vs. current date.
2. Check the `item_category` metadata.
3. Inspect `order_status` to ensure it was successfully delivered.
4. Review customer notes for mention of "opened" or "used" condition.
5. If the item is over $500, flag for supervisor approval.
6. For "Defective" claims, request and verify photo evidence before issuing a return label.
7. Confirm if the customer is a "Purple Prime" member for extended window eligibility.
