# Fraud and Safety Policy

## 1. Friendly Fraud Prevention
We monitor for patterns of "Empty Box" or "Item Not Received" claims. 
- **Action:** Accounts with a high rate of claims may be required to provide a police report for lost packages before a refund is issued.

## 2. Account Suspension
We reserve the right to suspend accounts that engage in harassment of support staff, excessive return abuse, or fraudulent payment disputes.

## 3. Identification Requirement
For refunds exceeding $500, we may require a valid government-issued ID to verify the account owner's identity.
