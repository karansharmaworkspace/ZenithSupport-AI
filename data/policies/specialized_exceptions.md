# Specialized Item Exceptions

## 1. Perishable Items (Food/Plants)
- **Melted Items:** We do not offer refunds for items that melt during transit if "Express Shipping" was not selected for perishable items.
- **Quality Issues:** If food arrives spoiled or damaged, photos must be provided within 24 hours for a refund. No returns are required.

## 2. Marketplace vs First-Party
- **First-Party (Sold by Us):** Fully covered by all internal policies.
- **Marketplace Seller:** Shipping and return policies are set by the seller. Our "Dispute Resolution" applies if the seller fails to comply with their stated policy.

## 3. Opened Items
- Electronics that have been opened and used are subject to a 15% restocking fee if returned within the 30-day window.
- Hygiene items (per the Returns Policy) cannot be returned once opened.
