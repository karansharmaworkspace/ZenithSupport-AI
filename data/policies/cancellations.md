# Cancellation Policy

## 1. Order Cancellation
Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement for a full refund.
- After 1 hour, the order may have entered the fulfillment process and cannot be cancelled.
- If an order has already shipped, it must be handled as a return.

## 2. Subscription Cancellations
Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time through the "My Account" portal.
- Monthly subscriptions: Cancellation will take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
- Annual subscriptions: No partial refunds for early cancellation.

## 3. Final Sale Cancellations
Items marked "Final Sale" cannot be cancelled once the order is placed.
