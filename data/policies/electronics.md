# Electronics and Technology Support

## 1. High-Value Electronics Verification
This policy applies to Laptops, Desktop Computers, Tablets, and Smartphones.
- **IMEI/Serial Check:** Every high-value device has its unique identifier (IMEI or Serial Number) scanned before shipping. 
- **Return Condition:** To prevent "swapping fraud," we verify the returned unit's identifier matches the shipped unit. Any mismatch will result in an immediate denial of refund and a permanent ban from the platform.

## 2. Lithium Battery Safety
Federal and international regulations prohibit the air transport of damaged or defective lithium batteries.
- **Policy:** If your device is experiencing "Battery Swelling" or "Thermal Runaway," DO NOT MAIL IT.
- **Action:** Contact support for a "Battery Safety Kit" or instructions on local hazardous material disposal. We will process your refund once proof of disposal is provided.

## 3. Data Erasure and Account Removal
Before returning any device with storage (including smart TVs and smart home hubs):
- **Requirement:** You MUST sign out of all Cloud accounts (iCloud, Google, Samsung, etc.).
- **Reset:** Perform a "Factory Reset" to wipe all personal data.
- **Failure to Comply:** Devices that are locked to a user's account will be shipped back at the owner's expense, and no refund will be issued.
