# Disputes and Resolutions

## 1. Incorrect Items Received
If you receive an item different from what you ordered (wrong SKU, color, or size):
- **Notification:** Inform us within 7 days of receipt.
- **Return:** We will provide a prepaid shipping label for the return of the incorrect item.
- **Replacement:** The correct item will be shipped immediately upon the carrier scanning the return package.

## 2. Missing Items from Bundle
If you ordered a bundle or a "Set" and one component is missing:
- **Resolution:** We will ship the missing component separately or offer a partial refund proportional to the value of the missing piece.

## 3. Marketplace Seller Disputes
For items purchased from third-party Marketplace Sellers:
- **First Step:** You must contact the seller directly through the "Contact Seller" button.
- **Seller Response:** Sellers have 2 business days to respond to your inquiry.
- **A-to-Z Guarantee:** If the seller does not respond or refuses a valid return within policy, you may file an "A-to-Z Guarantee" claim through the main Support dashboard.
- **Mediation:** Purple Merit acts as a mediator for all Marketplace disputes to ensure customer satisfaction.
