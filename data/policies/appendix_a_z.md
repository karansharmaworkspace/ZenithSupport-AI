# Multi-Category Detailed Policy Appendix (A-Z)
This appendix contains specific edge-case resolutions for every product sub-category. 
Agents must use the specific SKU-level or category-level code when processing returns for these items.

## Home Decor Policy Nuances
For items in the Home Decor category, the following rules apply:
- **Standard Window:** 30 days unless specified otherwise.
- **Condition Check:** Must be verified by the Home Decor specialist if value > $200.
- **Restocking Fee:** 15% if the Home Decor item was used even once.
- **Procedural Step:** Check for Home Decor-specific serial number tags.
- **Fraud Risk:** Moderate for Home Decor high-value SKUs.
- **Special Handling:** Do not stack Home Decor items with liquid products in transit.
- **Customer Expectation:** 3-5 business days for Home Decor inspection processing.

This Home Decor policy is updated as of March 2026. All agents are required to attend the bi-weekly Home Decor calibration meeting to ensure alignment on return conditions. Failure to follow Home Decor specific SOPs will result in audit deductions. For further details on Home Decor logistics, refer to the Logistics Master Manual Section HOM-99.
