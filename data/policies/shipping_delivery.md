# Shipping and Delivery Policy (Comprehensive)

## 1. Delivery Estimates
Shipping times are estimated and not guaranteed. Factors such as weather, carrier backlogs, and customs can affect these times.
- **Purple Economy Shipping:** 7-10 business days. Cost: Free for orders over $50.
- **Standard Ground Shipping:** 3-5 business days. 
- **Purple Priority Express:** 1-2 business days.
- **International Priority:** 7-14 business days.

## 2. Fulfillment Types
### 2.1 First-Party Items
Stored and shipped directly from Purple Merit distribution centers.
- **Packaging:** Purple-branded boxes with eco-friendly fillers.
- **Tracking:** Real-time updates every 12-24 hours.

### 2.2 Marketplace Seller Items
Stored and shipped by independent third-party sellers.
- **Packaging:** Varies by seller.
- **Tracking:** Provided by the seller. Purple Merit does not have direct control over carrier selection for these items.

## 3. Lost or Damaged Packages
If an item is marked as "Delivered" but you have not received it:
- **Search Period:** Please wait 48 hours as carriers sometimes mark packages as delivered prematurely.
- **Claim Window:** Claims for lost packages must be filed within 14 days of the expected delivery date.
- **Investigation:** We will launch a formal investigation with the carrier. This process takes 3-5 business days.
- **Resolution:** If the investigation confirms the package was not delivered, we will offer a free replacement or a full refund.

## 4. Damaged in Transit
If your package arrives visibly damaged:
- **Action:** Please take photos of the packaging and the damaged item immediately.
- **Reporting:** Report the damage to Support within 48 hours of receipt.
- **Evidence:** High-resolution photos are mandatory for all damage-related refund requests.

## 5. Shipping Insurance (Purple Guard)
All orders over $200 are automatically enrolled in "Purple Guard" insurance at no extra cost.
- **Coverage:** Replaces items damaged or lost in transit without a carrier investigation if the customer is a "Purple Prime" member.
- **Exclusions:** Does not cover "Theft after delivery" if the package was left in an unsecured location at the customer's request.

## 6. Hazardous Materials (HAZMAT)
Items containing lithium batteries, aerosols, or flammable liquids are subject to special shipping regulations.
- **Packaging:** Must use approved UN-rated packaging for returns.
- **Courier:** Can only be shipped via Ground services. Air transport is strictly prohibited for returns of damaged HAZMAT items.

## 4. Marketplace Sellers
Items fulfilled by third-party Marketplace Sellers follow the seller's specific shipping policy, which is displayed on the product page.
